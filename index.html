At first, I started as a kitchen hand just to cover my school fee, while having no goals for my life. The job changed me a lot from my personality to my approach towards life. After the course the part-time job turning into my full-time career as i started getting more serious towards learning more skill in cookery field.
Slowly moving up the kitchen ladder, in 2016 I am officaly a full-time chef. The variety of ingredients, the techniques behind a simple dish, the rush of adrenaline during service. I just couldn't get enough of it until one day i decided to switch my career.

I am on way to
become an IT worker

After the 3 years working as a chef, I would often question myself if this is the right career. I really enjoyed the job and the people in the industry. but my drive for cookery was deflating and i needed to find my new drive in a different career.
With some a lot of friends guidance, research and a few personality tests. It helped narrow down my interest in IT and this is when i started my new passion for software developement.With carefull research on Institutes throughout NZ, I enrolled at NMIT, starting course on the 20 of July 2020.
